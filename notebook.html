Rules of my life:

  1. In any situation, there are always 3 ways for action. Neither 1 nor 2, but 3. Heads, tails or edge.
  2. You should always help people. The time will come and you'll be in need for help too.
  3. Always leave tips to persons for their work. Don't leave without rewarding someone with tips who has worked for it.
  4. Drink more water.
  5. Life isn't a fairytale. The sooner you understand this - the easier you accept it.
  6. Wake up early. At 6:00 am. Be a nightingale.

  1. Plan your day. Make a plan for the nearest future. I mean days, week, month and year. Set goals and just do it.
  2. Read more. Develop your speech and your imagination. You must train your brain.
  3. Do sport every day. Surely, accompanieda by music.
  4. Clean your room. If you know how to make a mess, you must know how to clean it up. If you can't put things in order, how would you manage doing this with your life?
  5. Find a job you would like best. I mean your ideal job, a job that delights you. And remember, the best job is your hobby. Do what you love, love what you do and let it bring pleasure
  6. Don't listen to anyone. Do what you consider worth doing. Enjoy it, because this life is yours.
  7. If everyone drinks tea without sugar and you like sweet tea, add more sugar. Taste the life sweet.
  8. Be of your own view. Don't choose a convenient truth.
  9. If "Plan A" didn't work, you still have 26 letters. If they say that your train is gone, just remember you have besides an airplane , a helicopter, a car or even a bicycle. Remember when you were a baby and learned to walk. Your attempts had little success. You didn't give it up saying "This is not for me". Don't betray your dreams.
  10. Travel as much as possible. You have to see the whole world. Why did someone see the Eiffel Tower, and you didn’t? Why someone walked on the " Great Wall of China " but not you? We are often told to save money. But I think if you are not 40 years old this is all a bullshit. Invest in yourself. Spend all money on travel and buy clothes abroad. After all what difference does it make, how old your sneakers are, if you you are wearing them while walking in California?
  11. Never insult a person. No one knows what is in her heart and how she treats you.
  12. Before saying something think about your idea. Think twice.
  13. Take risks every day. Risk- is a life. If you don't take risk, you'll not win. The biggest risk -is to never risk anything in your life. Once you risk, you can win everything, but if you don't risk, you will lose 100%.
  14. Don't BE afraid of changes. Do something new. Can you remember me, the last time you did something new?
  15. Dream.
  16. Be a gentlemen in any situation
  17. Give your girlfriend chocolate. Any girl is likeas a child, and every child likesloves sweets. Winning the girl`s heart is not so difficult. You need TO BE able to do three things: to pity her, to pamper and to make her smile.
  18. Don't make promises if you know you can’t keep THEM.
  19. Never stop smiling, what if someone is looking at you right at the moment? Then he falls in love with your smile. He will remember you happy, not sad. Hmm someone might be taking A picture of you, then you have to keep your back erectly!
  20. Create new ideas, what if you are someone who will change this world? WHAT IF you are the one who will change "History" or write new stories . Or tear the hooks of stereotypes? And if they tell you are crazy, say them that you are a genius.
  21. Be smart, but don't try to be clever. Fool around, but don't be a fool.
  22. If you like a person, then what difference does it make how much money she has? Or what color is her skin?
  23. After all remember that there are no rules. Never get bogged
    Down under the law and the government. Free people build everything for themselves, only god can judge you. If you want to do something, just do it. And don't pay attention what they say to you.
  24. Never stop learning, because life never stops teaching.
